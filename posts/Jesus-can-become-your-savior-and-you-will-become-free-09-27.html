Jesus can become your savior and you will become free!

Child of God

Jesus is the Son of God, but he is also God. Indeed, Son of God means “from God”. God who is Spirit came to Earth in a body, in Jesus, namely that in Jesus, he made himself very small and came as a man. He agreed to die and was resurrected on the 3rd day. In this death and this suffering, he paid for your faults. He was condemned in your place, he who was perfect. To be saved, forgiven, reconciled with God, live a personal and strong relationship with him from this life, and spend your eternity in his love, you only have one thing to do. Recognize that you need Jesus, that you want him to bear your sins on the cross and cover you with his righteousness. So God will see you as righteous, he will become your Father, put his Spirit in you, and you will live the life you were created to live. Indeed, all the riches of this world, all the glory of this world, will never satisfy you. What you need is the love of your God who created you, so you will never be alone again. Read the Bible to learn more. I advise you to start with the New Testament, in particular with a Gospel... With all my love in Christ,
